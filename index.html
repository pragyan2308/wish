Happy Father's Day

Dear Papa,

On this Father's Day, I just want to say —
You are my first hero, my strongest shield, and my quietest support.
You believed in me when I didn’t believe in myself.
You stood behind me like a mountain, steady and unshakable.

You taught me more than just how to walk or talk —
You taught me how to face the world with courage, with kindness, and with heart.
You showed me that real strength is silent,
And love doesn’t always need words to be loud.

Thank you for your sacrifices — the ones you never speak of.
Thank you for your patience, your lessons, your hugs,
And every moment you chose me over your comfort.

I’m proud to be your child — always.

With all my love,
Your Little One 💙
